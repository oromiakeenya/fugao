Face Up Generation Aid Organization (FUGAO) , is aboardly Organization legally registered none for profit Organization (NGO) with a Registration No. 7569. Our organization is committed to Ending Extreme Poverty whatever it takes through empowering youth, fostering social cohesion, addressing pressing social challenges through Advocacy, capacity-building programs, leadership development, and community-driven initiatives.

Face up Generation Aid Organization (FUGAO) Striveto ensure community equitable access to assistance, protection, services, resources and opportunities needed to thrive, regardless of their socio-economic status,and become free from poverty and social exclusion.While seeking and advocating for durable solutions.

Key priority areas and strategic Objective of Face up Generation Aid Organization (FUGAO): include;

  • Provide knowledge and skills to enable inclusive and quality programming

  • Empower and support operational actors to localize responses

  • Collaborate with sectors and actors to improve field coordination

  • Enhance visibility and recognition of humanitarian preparedness, response, and recovery

  • Training services for cluster members, partners, authorities, and affected communities

  • Providing up-to-date, relevant Policy, tools, guidance and support for operational practitioners

  • Advocacy and resource mobilization to enhance the influence and improve coordination

Our mission is to empower communities through inclusive education, healthcare, economic development and social support to ensuring that every individual has the opportunity to reach their full potential.

Our vision is a thriving Ethiopia where every community is empowered, every individual is valued, and sustainable development is a reality for all, free from poverty and inequality.

Empowerment: Empowering individuals and communities to lead their own development.
Integrity: Maintaining transparency, honesty, and accountability in all our actions.
Sustainability: Focusing on long-term solutions that benefit both people and the environment.
Inclusivity: Ensuring equal opportunities and support for all, especially marginalized groups.

Face up Generation Aid Organization (FUGAO):works to enhance the resilience capacities of vulnerable populations to shocks and deliver integrated and holistic HDP (Humanitarian-Development-Peace) Programs to attain inclusive sustainable development.

  1. Humanitarian

    The Humanitarian response pillar aims to deliver lifesaving and timely support to the most in need populations in Ethiopia affected by on set and or protracted emergencies.

  2. Development

    Development programmers of Face Up Generation Aid Organization (FUGAO) intends to support targeted populations to attain sustainable development, ensure resilient capacities to potential shocks and empower beneficiaries as well as local structures to led or diversify development outcomes.

  3. Peace Building

    Peace Building is one of the programmer pillars of Face up Generation Aid Organization (FUGAO) which aligned with the Humanitarian response and development programs to ensure a sustainable change keeping peaceful coexistence and reduced risks towards conflict.
