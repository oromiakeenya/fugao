Face Up Generation Aid Organization(FUGAO)

Face Up Generation Aid Organization

Face Up Generation Aid Organization (FUGAO), is aboardledOrganizationlegally registered none for profit Organization (NGO) in Ethiopia with a Registration No. 7569. Our organization is committed to Ending Extreme Poverty whatever it takes through empowering youth, fostering social cohesion, addressing pressing social challenges through Advocacy, capacity-building programs, leadership development, and community-driven initiatives.

Our mission is to empower communities through inclusive education, healthcare, economic development and social support to ensuring that every individual has the opportunity to reach their full potential.

Our vision is a thriving Ethiopia where every community is empowered, every individual is valued, and sustainable development is a reality for all, free from poverty and inequality.

Our Thematic Area:-Humanitarian, Development, Peace Building, Protection, Advocacy, Education, Health, Women and youth economic Empowerment, Child protection, Lifesaving Emergency Response for (IDP), ES/NFI, Nutrition, WASH, Food Security, Livelihood, Capacity Building, Knowledge customization, Knowledge localization, Life skill and entrepreneurship

Our Goals and Objectives:- FUGAO Engage with more than ten issues that most matterincluding:- No poverty, Zero hunger, Good health and well-being, Quality education, Gender equality, Decent work and economic growth, Reduced inequalities, Sustainable cities and communities, Responsible production and consumption, and Partnerships for the common goals.

Our Initiatives

Education and Capacity Building: Offering educational opportunities, vocational training, and skills development for marginalized groups, with a focus on youth and women.

Health and Well-being:Providing access to basic healthcare services, sanitation, and nutrition programs to improve the health outcomes of underserved communities.

Economic Empowerment:Supporting small-scale businesses, entrepreneurs, and farmers through microfinance, job creation, and economic development programs.

Advocacy and Rights Protection:Promoting human rights, gender equality, and the protection of vulnerable populations through advocacy and legal support.

Sustainability and Environment:Engaging communities in sustainable agricultural practices, reforestation projects, and environmental education to safeguard the future
